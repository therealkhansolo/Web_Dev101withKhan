Must Watch Movies According to Khan

My top 3 of all time:

The Prestige(2006)

Set in 19th-century London, this film follows two rival magicians, Robert Angier and Alfred Borden, who engage in a fierce competition to create the ultimate illusion. Their obsession leads them to sacrifice everything, including their personal lives, in pursuit of supremacy in the world of magic.


The Dark Knight Rises(2012)

Eight years after the events of "The Dark Knight," Bruce Wayne has retired as Batman. He is forced to return when a new adversary, Bane, emerges, threatening Gotham City. As Bane's plan unfolds, Bruce must confront his past andembrace his role as a hero once more.


The Harder They Fall(2021)

A bold and stylish Western that delivers a fresh perspective with its all-Black cast and dynamic storytelling. Jonathan Majors and Idris Elba shine in their roles, bringing intensity and charisma to their characters. The film's direction by Jeymes Samuel is visually striking, blending modern sensibilities with classic Western elements. The soundtrack, curated by Samuel himself, is a standout, infusing the movie with a unique energy. The plot, centered around revenge and redemption, keeps viewers engaged with its twists and action-packed sequences. Overall, it's a thrilling and refreshing take on the Western genre that leaves a lasting impression.
